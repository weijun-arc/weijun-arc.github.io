Jun Wei (魏军)'s Homepage

I am currently an Assistant Professor affiliated with the Visual Computing Research Center (VCC) at the College of Computer Science & Software Engineering (CSSE), Shenzhen University (SZU).

Before coming to SZU, I obtained my PhD's degree under the supervision of Prof. Zhen Li in 2024 from the School of Science and Engineering (SSE) at The Chinese University of Hong Kong, Shenzhen (CUHKSZ). Prior to that, I received my Master's degree under the supervision of Prof. Shuhui Wang in 2020 from the Institute of Computing Technology (ICT), Chinese Academy of Sciences (CAS), and Bachelor's degree from the School of Electronic Information and Communications (SEIC), Huazhong University of Science and Technology (HUST) in 2017.

My research interests are mainly in computer vision and computer graphics, with a recent focus on Urban Scene Reconstruction.
